Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Scout Family Products ("we", "us", or "our") operates the ShirtScout mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the app:

  • Search queries — the terms you search for within the app.
  • Device information — basic device type and operating system version for crash reporting and performance monitoring.
  • Usage data — which features you use, to help us improve the app.

We do not require account creation. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

Affiliate Links

ShirtScout displays products from a network of retail partners. When you tap a product and visit a retailer's website, we may earn a small commission through affiliate programs. The price you pay is never affected by this. Retailers may set their own cookies when you visit their sites — please refer to each retailer's privacy policy for details.

Third-Party Services

ShirtScout connects to a growing network of retail partners and service providers. These third parties may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any retailer you visit through our app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated usage data to improve our services.

Data Retention

Search queries and usage data are retained for up to 90 days for performance analysis, then deleted.

Children's Privacy

ShirtScout is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
