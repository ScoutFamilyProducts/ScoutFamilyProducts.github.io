How it works

ShopScout is a mobile shopping discovery app. Users browse product modules, compare normalized results, and complete purchases directly with the retailer or marketplace.

Data sourcing

ShopScout does not scrape merchant websites. Product data and outbound links are sourced through approved affiliate programs, product feeds, APIs, and network-provided tracking links.
