Our approach

Online shopping rewards the patient. Open one tab for a marketplace, another for the brand's own store, a third to compare prices, a fourth to read reviews — and somewhere along the way, the thing you wanted slips out of reach.

Scout Family Products takes the work out of that ritual. Our apps query trusted retailers in parallel, normalize the results into a single coherent view, and let you make a decision the way it should be made: by looking at the options, side by side, on a screen that respects your time.

What we believe

  • The search should serve the shopper. Results are organized to be useful, not to maximize anyone's commission.
  • Transparency is non-negotiable. When we earn from an affiliate link, we say so. When pricing changes between our app and the retailer's checkout, we tell you to verify there.
  • Mobile-first, deeply native. No interstitials, no hijacked browsers, no cluttered toolbars. Tap a result and you arrive on the retailer's own product page.
  • Refinement matters. Software, like anything else worth keeping, can be made with care.

What we're building

Our flagship is ShopScout, a mobile shopping app launching on Google Play. ShopScout begins with three categories — shirts, shoes, and headwear — each served by its own dedicated module within the app: ShirtScout, ShoeScout, and LidScout.

Behind the scenes, a unified connector layer brings together major marketplaces alongside category-specific brand partners, so a single search in ShopScout returns the breadth of a marketplace and the depth of a specialty store at the same time.
