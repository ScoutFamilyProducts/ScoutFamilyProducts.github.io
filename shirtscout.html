Buying Guide

How to find the right shirt

Casual T-Shirts

For everyday t-shirts, eBay is often the strongest source — both new retail stock and discounted branded inventory. Look for listings marked "Brand New" with free returns for the safest purchases. Amazon's marketplace depth is also strong for basics from established brands. Filter by size early to avoid browsing inventory that won't fit.

Dress & Formal Shirts

Formal occasions and office wear benefit from ShirtScout's specialty connector. AW Bridal carries an extensive range of formal shirts and occasion wear at competitive price points. For standard dress shirts, filtering by brand on Amazon often surfaces the best balance of price and quality. Pay attention to collar type and sleeve length when comparing across retailers.

Hoodies & Sweatshirts

The hoodie market is one of the most competitive in apparel. ShirtScout's cross-retailer search is particularly effective here because the same style often varies $20–40 in price between platforms. Sort by Lowest Price first, then verify the seller's return policy before purchasing.

Graphic & Novelty Tees

eBay's seller ecosystem is unmatched for graphic tees, vintage designs, and novelty shirts. ShirtScout surfaces these alongside new retail stock so you can compare print-on-demand options with established sellers. Check seller feedback scores for graphic tee purchases.

How it works

One search. Multiple sources. Better results.

ShirtScout is a module within the ShopScout mobile app. Open the app, navigate to ShirtScout, type your search, and results from all connected retailers appear in a single normalized feed — sorted by your preferred criteria.

Step 02

Step 03

Tap any result to go directly to the retailer's site. Your purchase is completed securely with the retailer — not through us.
